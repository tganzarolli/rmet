INTRODUCTION

You're about to participate in a research study. Your contribution to our research allows us to learn more about how people recognize emotions of others.
Please, read the following information carefully before proceeding.

What is it about

This test will investigate your ability to read emotion from the eyes. There is one practice question followed by 36 test questions.

What you will have to do

You will be shown 37 pictures showing just the eyes part of people's faces. You will be asked to guess what emotion these eyes are showing.

Duration

Approximately 6 minutes.
